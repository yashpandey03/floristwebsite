About Us

WhatMotivates Us

Flowers are the best gift of nature to mankind that help us to express several emotions without speaking a single word. Bringing this beauty of nature close to you is "Kelivana", a prominent Florist & a leading Floral Arrangement Service Provider in Delhi.

  • From humble beginnings to floral mastery. Bringing beauty to Delhi and NCR with our exquisite arrangements.
  • Today, while continuing to sell fresh-cut flowers at our premises, we offer a wide selection of unique creations, made with fresh or artificial flowers, for the home and the office.
  • Over the years, these floral arrangements have also helped our customers to express special messages to their loved ones, friends, and business associates where words may sometimes be inadequate.

Shop flowers at your fingertips. Kelivana combines online ordering with our renowned service and expertise.
